Usage: privacy-pools withdraw quote [options] <amountOrAsset> [amount]

Request relayer quote and limits without generating a proof

Arguments:
  amountOrAsset       Amount to withdraw (preferred first positional) or asset
                      symbol
  amount              Asset symbol (preferred second positional,
                      case-insensitive) or amount when the asset is first

Options:
  -t, --to <address>  Recipient address (recommended for an accurate fee quote)
  -h, --help          display help for command
Preferred order: privacy-pools withdraw quote <amount> <asset>
Also supported: privacy-pools withdraw quote <asset> <amount>

Tip: add --help-full for examples, safety notes, and JSON fields.
